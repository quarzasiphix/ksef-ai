### Potential Concerns

1. **"This helps companies hide income"**
   - **Response:** No. Final invoice still goes to KSeF. We just reduce errors before submission.

2. **"This delays KSeF submission"**
   - **Response:** Agreement takes 1-2 days. Corrections in KSeF take weeks and create audit flags.

   - **Response:** No. This is pre-accounting. Like contract negotiation before signing.

4. **"Tax office won't like this"**
   - **Response:** Tax office prefers clean submissions over correction trails. We help compliance.

### Compliance Features to Emphasize

- **Audit trail** - Every negotiation step logged
- **Timestamps** - When agreement was reached
- **Both parties visible** - No one-sided changes
- **Final KSeF submission** - Nothing bypassed
- **Reduction in corrections** - Better for everyone
