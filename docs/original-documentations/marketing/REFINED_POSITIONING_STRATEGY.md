# Refined Positioning Strategy: Polish Economic Agency

## Core Positioning Spine (Internal Law)

```
KsięgaI istnieje po to, aby wzmacniać polskich przedsiębiorców — 
również wtedy, gdy działają międzynarodowo.
```

**This sentence resolves everything:**
- ✅ Polish identity
- ✅ Cross-border reality
- ✅ No contradiction
- ✅ No defensiveness

**You're not helping "foreigners". You're helping Poles operating abroad.**

---

## Strategic Shift: From Exclusion to Empowerment

### Before (Too Narrow):
> "Tylko polskie firmy"
> "Wyłącznie w Polsce"

### After (Confident & Inclusive):
> "Dla polskich przedsiębiorców — także tych, którzy sprzedają za granicę"
> "W kraju i za granicą"

**Impact:** Maintains Polish center of gravity while acknowledging reality of cross-border operations.

---

## Language Discipline (Non-Negotiable)

### Polish-First Rules:
- 100% Polish UI and copy
- No English words (except: AI, KSeF, VAT, NIP)
- No startup-bro tone
- Slightly formal, but human
- Selling stability, not disruption

### When Mentioning International Features:
- Always frame as "dla polskich firm"
- Never use "global" or "international" alone
- Always tie back to Polish compliance
- Use specific countries (NL, DE) not "world"
